Recipe: Beachside Seafood Pasta

Ingredients:

1 lb. spaghetti
2 tbsp. olive oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 small onion, finely chopped
1/2 cup white wine
1 lb. mixed seafood (shrimp, mussels, clams, calamari, etc.)
1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
Salt and pepper to taste
Lemon wedges, for serving

Instructions:

Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add spaghetti and cook until al dente, about 8-10 minutes.

While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and onion and 
sauté until softened, about 3-5 minutes.

Add the white wine to the skillet and let it simmer for a minute or two to reduce slightly.

Add the mixed seafood to the skillet and sauté until cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Discard any unopened mussels or clams.

Drain the spaghetti and add it to the skillet with the seafood. Toss well to combine.

Season the dish with salt and pepper to taste.

Garnish with chopped parsley and serve with lemon wedges on the side.
